Rogue / Paladin / Sorceress

Instructions

This game is a lot like the old classic Rock, Paper, Scissors, but with a medieval twist! Click the "Start Game" button below to begin the game.

  1. Click "Start Game" when ready to begin.
  2. Click on the Rogue, Paladin, or Sorceress to select it as your choice of play. The computer will also choose one of the three, at random.
    • Rogue > Sorceress
    • Sorceress > Paladin
    • Paladin > Rogue
  3. Read the announcement to see who wins the round.
  4. The first to 5 rounds won wins the whole game.
